About

Tristan de Kock

I'm an AI Automation Engineer based in Cape Town. Day job: building production agentic systems and LLM pipelines at PerformanceX and Digital Authority Partners. Stack: Anthropic Claude SDK, Modal, Supabase, n8n, Python, TypeScript.

Custos is a side practice I'm building around a specific problem: South African enterprises in regulated industries need to deploy production AI on sovereign infrastructure, and most of them don't yet have the architectural depth or a trustworthy local partner to help them do it correctly.

POPIA Section 72 restricts cross-border transfers of personal information. For health data, financial data, and biometrics, the default cloud AI deployment pattern (OpenAI through Azure, routed via US data centres) creates real compliance obligations most teams haven't addressed. Cassava AI Factory changed the infrastructure equation in October 2025. The architecture problem is what's left to solve.

What I'm working on

Currently: reading POPIA end to end, building production RAG pipelines over compliance documents, benchmarking vLLM on rented H100s, and writing about what I learn.

Next: CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator), Terraform Associate, and AI-102. Then building toward Cassava Cloud Partner status with a real SA case study as proof.

The venture

Custos is not yet a registered Pty Ltd. I'm validating demand before formalising the business, which is deliberate. The first product is a Sovereign AI Readiness Assessment — a 2 to 3 week engagement that maps AI workloads, identifies POPIA exposure, and designs a sovereign deployment path.

If you're a CIO, CRO, or Head of Data at a SA medical aid, bank, or insurer, and you've been asked "where does our AI data live?" without a clean answer, that's the conversation I want to have. 25 minutes. No pitch.
